<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your desired store under Stores.">
    ![appstics-db-2.png](/images/appstics-db-2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Languages tab in the menu. Click on Add new language.">
    ![Anl (3) Upscaled](/images/anl\(3\)upscaled.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your desired language in the drop-down and click on Add new language.">
    ![Anl (1) Upscaled](/images/anl\(1\)upscaled.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="You can easilly edit the language fields by entering your desired text. Once you are finished click on Save or Save and close.">
    ![Anl (2) Upscaled](/images/anl\(2\)upscaled.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="If your language translation is not available, you can refer to the next chapter to learn [https://docs.apptics.ai/Using-the-Languages-Feature/Translating-languages-using-AI).">
    Note: The language displayed at checkout is based on your customer’s IP address. For example, if a customer is located in Norway, the checkout will automatically appear in Norwegian — provided that you’ve added Norwegian in your Language settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can add multiple languages to your store and customize each one individually. JSON mode allows you to easily translate content into languages not currently supported by using AI.
