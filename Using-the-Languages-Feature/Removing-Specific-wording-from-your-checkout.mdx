    ![rswf (2).jpg](/images/rswf\(2\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the text as needed, then click Save and Close.">
    ![rswf (3).jpg](/images/rswf\(3\).jpg)

  <Step title="Preview the checkout again to see your changes.">
