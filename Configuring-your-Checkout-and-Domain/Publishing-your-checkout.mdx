<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your desired store under Stores.">
    ![Pyc(3) Upscaled](/images/pyc\(3\)upscaled.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Checkout and click on Publish to Shopify.">
    ![Pyc(4) Upscaled](/images/pyc\(4\)upscaled.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="A side menu will appear, allowing you to configure how Apptics integrates with your Shopify store. ">
    ![Pyc(1) Upscaled](/images/pyc\(1\)upscaled.png)

    There, you can choose to enable or disable the Checkout Button Detector, Buy Now Button Detector, and set the percentage of customers directed to the Apptics checkout versus the Shopify native checkout.

    You’ll also find options to enable the Tracker, Caching Bundle Mode, and configure URL Patterns.

    Each of these settings will be explained in more detail in the following chapters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on Continue to publish your checkout with the settings you have selected.">
    ![Pyc(2) Upscaled](/images/pyc\(2\)upscaled.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

To publish your checkout, you’ll first need to deploy it. Click [here](https://docs.apptics.ai/Configuring-your-Checkout-and-Domain/Deploying-your-Checkout) to learn more about how to deploy your checkout.
