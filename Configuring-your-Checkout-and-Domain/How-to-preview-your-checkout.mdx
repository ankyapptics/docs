---
title: "How to preview your checkout"
description: "You can preview your checkout while customizing it, allowing you to fine-tune the experience before publishing it."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your desired store under Stores.">
    ![htp (5).jpg](/images/htp\(5\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Checkout and click on Preview Checkout.">
    ![htp (4).jpg](/images/htp\(4\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="A pop-up will appear, allowing you to choose a product for the checkout preview, as well as select the language and currency.">
    ![htp (1).jpg](/images/htp\(1\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Once you've made your selections, click on Preview Checkout.">
    ![htp (2).jpg](/images/htp\(2\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="A screen will appear displaying the exact checkout experience you’ve set up for your store. You can preview both the desktop and mobile versions, and also choose to open the preview in a new window.">
    ![htp (3).jpg](/images/htp\(3\).jpg)

    In the next chapter, you'll learn how to [Publish your checkout](https://docs.apptics.ai/Configuring-your-Checkout-and-Domain/Publishing-your-checkout).
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can preview your checkout while customizing it, allowing you to fine-tune the experience before publishing it.

To preview your checkout, you’ll first need to deploy it. Click [here](https://docs.apptics.ai/Configuring-your-Checkout-and-Domain/Deploying-your-Checkout) to learn more about how to deploy your checkout.
