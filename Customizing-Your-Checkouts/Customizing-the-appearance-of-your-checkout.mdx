---
title: "Customizing the Appearance of your Checkout"
description: "You can edit the appearance of your checkout by clicking on the Checkout tab in the menu of your Admin panel of the selected store."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click on Checkout, then click on Edit Apparence.">
    ![ctao (5).jpg](/images/ctao\(5\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Under Appearance, you can choose between a one-page or multi-page checkout. You’ll also be able to select the logo and favicon that will appear on your checkout, as well as customize the checkout color. Additionally, you have the option to enable or disable the checkout title and subtitle. ">
    ![ctao (1).jpg](/images/ctao\(1\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Under the Behavior section, you can choose which fields are required at checkout, such as phone number, address number, etc. You can also enable or disable the pre-checked marketing opt-in and the scarcity timer.">
    ![ctao (2).jpg](/images/ctao\(2\).jpg)

    Note: We recommend always enabling the “Sticky Payment Button on Mobile” option to ensure a smoother and more accessible checkout experience for mobile users.

    Note: We recommend enabling “Restrict Countries from Shipping Rates” if you want to limit shipping to fewer than 5 countries.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Under the Legal section, you can enter the URLs of your Terms & Conditions and Privacy policy. You can also disable or enable the option to require terms acceptance.5">
    ![ctao (3).jpg](/images/ctao\(3\).jpg)

    Note: We recommend disabling the “Require Terms Acceptance” button to provide a smoother checkout experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Under the Branding section, you can further customize your checkout by disabling or enabling the ‘Powered by Apptics’ option or adding logos for payment methods.">
    ![ctao (4).jpg](/images/ctao\(4\).jpg)

    Once you are done customizing your checkout don’t forget to click on the Save changes button.

    In the next chapters you can learn how to customize your checkout step by step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can edit the apparence of your checkout by clicking on the Checkout tab in the menu of your Admin panel of the selected store.
