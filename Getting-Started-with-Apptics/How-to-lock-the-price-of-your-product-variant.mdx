title: "How to lock the price of your product (variant)"
---


<Steps>
  <Step title="You can lock prices in different currencies for your products. To do this, click on the three dots next to your product and select Edit.">
    ![Hts 1 Upscaled](/images/hts1upscaled.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Next to the variant you would like to edit, click on the three dots and select Edit.">
    ![Hts 2 Upscaled](/images/hts2upscaled.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the currency you want to lock, enter your desired value, and then click the Save Changes button.">
    ![Hts 3 Upscaled](/images/hts3upscaled.png)

    This allows you to display the price on your website in the main website currency (in this example, USD), while charging your clients in EUR for the selected amount.
  </Step>
</Steps>
