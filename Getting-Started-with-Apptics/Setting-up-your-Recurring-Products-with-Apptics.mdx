---
title: "Setting up your Recurring Products with Apptics"
description: "If you have recurring sales in your store, you can add a recurring product directly in Apptics."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="To do this, click on the Products tab in the menu, then click Add.">
    ![suy (5).jpg](/images/suy\(5\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the name of the product for example “Your Store Club”. Disable the “is shippable” option.">
    ![suy 1.jpg](/images/suy1.jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on Add variant and enter the name of the product variant for exemple “Special Offer”. Upload an image of the product. Click on Add price.">
    ![suy (2).jpg](/images/suy\(2\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="In the drop down menu click on Recurring. Enter the price. Click on Add product button on the bottom right.">
    ![suy (4).jpg](/images/suy\(4\).jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="You have succesfully added your recurring product which is visible under products.">
    ![suy (3).jpg](/images/suy\(3\).jpg)

    In the next chapter, you’ll learn how to [lock the price of your product](https://docs.apptics.ai/Getting-Started-with-Apptics/How-to-lock-the-price-of-your-product-variant), if needed.

    Following that, the guide will walk you through [customizing your shipping rates](https://docs.apptics.ai/Getting-Started-with-Apptics/Customizing-Shipping-Rates).
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you have recurring sales in your store, you can add a recurring product directly in Apptics.
